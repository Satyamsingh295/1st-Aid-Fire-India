About 1stAidFire-India

Protecting Lives & Property Since 2001

1stAidFire-India is a premier fire safety solutions provider committed to protecting lives and property through innovative prevention, protection, and response systems.

Established in the Year 2001, 1st Aid Fire - INDIA, with the extreme efforts of Mr. Sahil Kaura we are today leading suppliers of Fire Safety Products & Equipments in all over INDIA. We are the channel partner or authorized distributors, dealers of various reputed companies.

Fire doesn't discriminate - neither should your safety measures. We bring enterprise-grade protection to businesses of all sizes.

Our team of certified professionals combines technical expertise with practical experience to deliver customized solutions that meet regulatory requirements and exceed safety standards.

Under the guidance of our visionary leadership team, 1stAidFire-India has consistently pushed the boundaries of fire safety innovation.

True leadership in fire safety means anticipating risks before they become emergencies.

Our executives bring decades of combined experience from military, industrial, and municipal firefighting backgrounds, ensuring a comprehensive approach to safety.

Safety isn't just our business - it's our core value. We implement rigorous safety protocols across all operations.

Compliance is the minimum. Excellence is our standard in every safety measure we implement.

Our policies are regularly updated to reflect the latest NFPA, BIS, and international standards, ensuring your protection is always current with best practices.
